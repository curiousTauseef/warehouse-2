Finish sale

Use the Finish sale window to save a sale and select a payment method. Payment can be done in cash, via debit/credit card, by vaucher or via bank account.

The application automatically calculates the change that the customer must receive.

Check the Print cash receipt option to send the data to a fiscal printer and to print a fiscal note.

Click the OK button to confirm the payment. Click Cancel to exit without saving the operation.
