Reprint Proforma Invoice

Use the Reprint Proforma Invoice window (keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Shift+F2) to reprint an existing proforma invoice. 

In the Select Document window select the document you wish to reprint and click OK.

In the Date range field you may select the date range for which you wish to visualize issued documents Today, Yesterday, This week, Past week, Last week, This month, Past month, Last month, This year, Past year, Last year or Custom. The Date filter is active only when you select Custom and you may enter the desired start and end date.

The Filter option allows you to quickly position on a particular document by typing its number or by typing all or part of the location�s name. To remove specified filter click on the Clear button.

When you save the operation, the application may offer you to print the sales document, depending on the Print Settings.
